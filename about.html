About Us

Learn more about our story and passion for Mediterranean cuisine.

Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant located in the heart of Chicago, Illinois. Founded by two brothers, Mario and Adrian, our restaurant offers a unique blend of traditional Italian, Greek, and Turkish dishes with a modern twist. Our chefs are dedicated to providing an authentic Mediterranean dining experience, using fresh and high-quality ingredients.
